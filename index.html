About Me

Hi, I'm Aaron Chen! I'm currently a student at Northeastern University, originally from California. Through my website, I'd love to share my photography journey with you. My dad was a photographer growing up and passed on his love for taking pictures to me. Ever since I was a child I've been fascinated by how pictures can capture a moment and tell stories in ways that words simply cannot. I mainly take film photography, and love experimenting with different film stocks.
